Privacy Policy for HoroNexus Mobile App

Effective Date: 10/10/2023

Introduction

Welcome to HoroNexus, the mobile app that allows you to explore your destiny with astrology and numerology. At HoroNexus, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information: We may collect personal information, such as your name and email address, when you voluntarily provide it to us during account creation or interactions with our app.
  2. Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our app, including the features you access and the content you interact with. This information helps us improve our services and user experience.
  3. Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including its type, operating system, and unique identifiers. This information helps us optimize our app for your device.

How We Use Your Information

  1. To Provide Services: We use your information to provide you with access to our app and its features, including personalized content and predictions based on astrology and numerology.
  2. To Improve Our App: We analyze user interactions to enhance our app's functionality, content, and user experience.
  3. To Communicate: We may send you notifications, updates, and promotional information about HoroNexus, but only with your consent.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information with service providers and partners who help us deliver and improve our services. We ensure they protect your information and use it only for the purposes specified.

Data Security

We employ reasonable security measures to safeguard your information. However, please be aware that no method of data transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Your Choices

You can update your account information and communication preferences by accessing your profile within the app. You may also opt out of receiving promotional communications from us at any time.

Children's Privacy

HoroNexus is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us to request its removal.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. You will be notified of any material changes. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the privacy of your information, please contact us at submiting a Feature Request inside the App.
